How to Prevent COVID-19

To prevent the spread of COVID-19, follow these guidelines:

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Wear a mask in public places where social distancing is difficult.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet of distance from others.
  • Avoid crowded places and close contact with people who are sick.
  • Disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces daily.
  • Stay at home if you're feeling unwell or have any symptoms.
